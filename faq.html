What are the main types of cryptocurrency?
Coins (Own blockchain):
- Bitcoin (BTC): Store of value
- Ethereum (ETH): Smart contracts
- Litecoin (LTC): Fast transactions
- Ripple (XRP): Cross-border payments

Tokens (Built on blockchains):
- ERC-20 (Ethereum): Utility/governance
- BEP-20 (BSC), NFTs

Stablecoins: USDT, USDC, BUSD (pegged to fiat)
Privacy Coins: Monero (XMR), ZCash (ZEC)
DeFi Tokens: UNI, AAVE, COMP
What are the main ways to use or engage with cryptocurrency?
Earning/Investing:
- Buy & hold, staking, yield farming
- Airdrops, trading (spot & futures)

Transactions:
- Peer-to-peer (P2P), remittances
- Buy goods/services online

Development:
- Build dApps, mint NFTs
- Create smart contracts (Solidity, Vyper)
